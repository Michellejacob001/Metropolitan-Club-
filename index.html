About

The perfect place for relaxation ,recreation and entertainment.

"Your perfect home way from home" set in the out heart of North Paravur but tucked away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Metrolpolitan Club is a private social club making more contributions in the field of entertainment of social and community services and on the other part we are entertaining our members and guests from 2007 by the dedication of 35 founder members.The same hospitality continues today as the club providing dinning,fitness center,swimming pool, wooden shuttle court,billards,card room and brilliant lounge for re-creation of children and family members.Also having msny associated social club of Kerala.It offers a refined clubbing experience to the members and family members

Metrolpolitan Club is the best place to unwind,recharge and indulge.
